Queues

Abstract data structures (FIFO)

queues:

  • Conceptual - Multiple ways of implementing a queue.
  • Last item inserted is the last removed.
  • Seen in managing background tasks or uploading resources.
  • Seen in printing and task processing.
  • Arrays CAN function as queues (push/shift or unshift/pop, but not mixed)

Big O

  • Insertion - O(1)
  • Removal - O(1)
  • Search - O(N)
  • Access - O(N)
